About Treep

An engaging 2D rogue-like set in a world of desperate insects

In Treep, you assume the role of the spirit of an insect colony residing at the base of a magnificent oak tree, threatened by a devastating fungal parasite. Delve into the depths of the tree, confront corrupted insects, and fight to save your colony. Each demise strengthens your foe, yet also provides an opportunity to be reborn with greater power.

Captivating rogue-like experience

Enjoy procedurally generated levels that present unique challenges in every playthrough.

2D Exploration

Navigate through the three main areas of the tree (root, trunk, canopy) and battle formidable bosses.

Sacrifice and strategy

Each death penalizes the player by transforming their former body into a relentless enemy.

Immersive multiplayer

Collaborate with up to five players, each with distinct roles and abilities to tackle challenges together.

Dynamic AI

Encounter enemies that adapt to your actions and combat situations.

Immersive universe

Experience 2D graphics in 32x32 pixels and an original soundtrack that alternates between soothing melodies and epic compositions.

Join the adventure and save the colony!
The survival of the colony depends on your choices, sacrifices, and your ability to explore a mysterious and unforgiving world.
