Chronology

The Treep project was born on a stormy night in early winter.

In Treep, you assume the role of the spirit of an insect colony residing at the base of a magnificent oak tree, threatened by a devastating fungal parasite. Delve into the depths of the tree, confront corrupted insects, and fight to save your colony. Each demise strengthens your foe, yet also provides an opportunity to be reborn with greater power.
